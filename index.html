Welcome to the Putt Putt Clubhouse

How to play:

1. Putt the ball into the hole with the fewest amount of strokes as possible

2. Be sure to lookout for the walls or the obstacles!

3. Use the keys listed below to manage your putt velocity

- ↔ control the direction of the putt

- SPACE hold down the spacebar to load the power

- Z lessen the amount of power

- ↑ or ↵ putt the ball!!
